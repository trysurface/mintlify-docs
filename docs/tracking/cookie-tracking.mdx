## Enable Cookie Tracking in Surface Form

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/cookie-tracking/1.png" alt="Surface form cookie tracking settings" />
</Frame>

1. **Open Form Settings**
   - Go to your Surface form dashboard
   - Navigate to the form you want to enable cookie tracking for
   - Click on **Settings** -> **Cookie Tracking**

2. **Enable Cookie Tracking**
   - Toggle the **Enable Cookie Tracking** switch to turn it on
   - Click **Save** to apply the changes
   - The form will now track the lead's cookies during form submission
