This guide shows you how to run **HubSpot email sequences** when leads submit your Surface form—including when they **drop off** partway through. As soon as a lead submits (even partially), they are enrolled into your HubSpot sequence and receive your automated follow-up emails, with optional personalization and meeting links.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Form submission">
    A lead submits your Surface form (partial or completed). Surface sends the submission to HubSpot via the **Create Form Response** workflow action.
  </Step>
  <Step title="HubSpot form response">
    HubSpot records the form submission for the HubSpot form you configured in your Surface workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automation enrolls in sequence">
    A HubSpot automation triggers on "Contact submits a specific form" and enrolls the contact in your sequence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email sequence">
    The contact receives your sequence emails (with delays and optional personalization and meeting links) according to the sequence you built.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A **Surface** account with a form that captures at least email (e.g., multi-step form where leads can drop off after the first step)
- A **HubSpot** account with **Sales Hub** (or higher) for Sequences and **Marketing Hub** for forms and automations
- In [Step 5](/guides/email-sequences-hubspot/step-5-surface-workflow) you'll connect Surface to HubSpot and set up a workflow that sends form submissions using **Create Form Response**. For more options, see [Configure HubSpot in Surface Workflow](/guides/hubspot-forms/configure-workflow) in the HubSpot forms guide.

<Info>
For **dropped-off** leads to be enrolled, your Surface workflow (Step 5) must use the **Dropped Off** trigger and map fields to the same HubSpot form used in the automation. Use **Finished** if you only want to enroll completed submissions.
</Info>
