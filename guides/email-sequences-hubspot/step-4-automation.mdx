In this step, you'll create a **HubSpot automation** that triggers when a contact submits the HubSpot form you use with Surface. The automation will **enroll the contact in your sequence** so they receive the emails you set up in Steps 2 and 3.

## Create the Automation

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/email-sequences-hubspot/9.png" alt="Automate tab – Create an automation" />
</Frame>

1. In your sequence, open the **Automate** tab
2. Click **Create an automation**

## Set the Trigger: Form Submission

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/email-sequences-hubspot/10.png" alt="Trigger: Contact submits a specific form" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/email-sequences-hubspot/11.png" alt="Enroll contact in this sequence" />
</Frame>

1. Set the trigger to **Form submission**
2. Choose **Contact submits a specific form**
3. Select the **HubSpot form** you created in [Step 1](/guides/email-sequences-hubspot/step-1-hubspot-form) (the same form your Surface workflow sends to via Create Form Response)

<Warning>
The form you select here must be the **exact same form** that your Surface workflow uses in the **Create Form Response** action. Otherwise, submissions from Surface won't trigger this automation.
</Warning>

## Configure the Enrollment Action

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/email-sequences-hubspot/12.png" alt="Enroll contact in this sequence" />
</Frame>

1. Add an action and choose **Enroll contact in this sequence** (or the equivalent sequence enrollment action)
2. Configure **sender email** and **sender name** so emails come from the right address and identity
3. Review any other options (e.g., eligibility, max enrollments) if your HubSpot plan supports them

## Create and Turn On the Automation

1. Click **Create** (or **Save**) to create the automation
2. Turn the automation **on** so it runs when the form is submitted

After this, any contact who submits that form—whether from a Surface **Dropped Off** or **Form Submission** workflow—will be enrolled in the sequence and receive your automated emails according to the steps and delays you configured.
