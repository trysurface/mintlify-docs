This guide shows you how to add a hidden "partial" status field to your existing HubSpot form. This approach is ideal if you have workflows already connected to your form and want to use the same form for both complete and partial submissions.

## When to Use This Option

This option is recommended if:
- You have existing workflows connected to your HubSpot form
- You want to use the same form for both complete and partial submissions
- You prefer minimal changes to your current setup
- You want to maintain a single source of truth for form submissions

## Step 1: Access Your HubSpot Form

1. Log in to your **HubSpot** account
2. Navigate to **Marketing** â†’ **Forms**
3. Click on the form you want to modify

## Step 2: Create a new Hidden Field

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/existing-form/1.png" alt="HubSpot form add field option" />
</Frame>

1. In your form editor, click **Create new** button
2. Select **Single-line text** from the field types
3. Configure the field settings:
   - **Object type**: `Contact`
   - **Field label**: `Submission Type`
   - **Internal name**: `submission_type`
4. Click **Create property**

## Step 3: Configure the Hidden Field

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/hubspot/existing-form/2.png" alt="HubSpot hidden field configuration" />
</Frame>

1. Click on the hidden field you just added
2. Configure the field settings:
   - **Make this field hidden**: Ensure this is set to **On**
3. Click **Save**
