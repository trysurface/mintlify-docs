Surface Forms integrates seamlessly with HubSpot to automatically create and update contacts, companies, and deals from your form submissions.

## Integration Steps

<Frame caption="HubSpot Integration">
    <img src="/images/integrations/hubspot/1.png" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to the **Integrations** panel from the sidebar
  2. Locate the HubSpot card and click **Authenticate**
  3. Select your HubSpot organization when prompted
  4. Accept Surface Labs invite
  5. Click **Connect App** to complete setup
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Card title="Integration Tips">
  <ol>
    <li>1. Map all relevant form fields to HubSpot properties</li>
    <li>2. Set up clear naming conventions</li>
    <li>3. Test the integration with sample submissions</li>
    <li>4. Monitor data flow regularly</li>
    <li>5. Keep field mappings updated</li>
  </ol>
</Card>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connection Issues">
    1. Verify HubSpot authentication
    2. Check organization permissions
    3. Reconnect integration if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Data Not Syncing">
    1. Confirm field mappings
    2. Check for required fields
    3. Verify form submission data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
