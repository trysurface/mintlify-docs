Slack is a popular messaging platform that allows you to communicate with your team and customers. Surface Forms can be integrated with Slack to send notifications when a form is submitted, in progress or dropped.

## Integration Steps

<Frame caption="Slack Integration">
    <img src="/images/integrations/slack/1.png" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
  1. Navigate to the **Integrations** panel from the sidebar
  2. Find the Slack card and click **Authenticate**
  3. Select your Slack workspace and authorize with required permissions
  4. Click **Install** to complete the basic setup
</Steps>

## Private Channel Configuration

For private channels, you need to add the Surface Bot to the channel.

<Steps>
  - Open the channel where you want to receive notifications <br/>
  - Add Surface app to the channel using:
    <Frame caption="Add Surface app to the channel">
        <img src="/images/integrations/slack/2.png" />
    </Frame>
  - Select "Surface" from the apps list
    <Frame caption="Select Surface app">
        <img src="/images/integrations/slack/3.png" />
    </Frame>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Make sure you have the necessary permissions in your Slack workspace to add apps to channels.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bot Not Posting">
    1. Verify bot is invited to the channel
    2. Check channel permissions
    3. Confirm Surface app is added to channel
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing Notifications">
    1. Verify workflow configuration
    2. Check notification settings
    3. Ensure correct channel selection
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
