---
title: How to Install on Your Site
description: Learn how to install the Surface Tag on your site to enhance your forms with advanced tracking and analytics capabilities.
---

Add the Surface Tag to your website by including this script in your HTML:

```html
<!-- Start Surface Tag -->
<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/trysurface/scripts@latest/surface_tag.min.js"></script>
<!-- End Surface Tag -->
```
